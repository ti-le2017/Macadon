PARTY FAVORS


 Macaron favors are a great treat for your wedding or party guests. Available in a wide range of colors and flavors, our macarons can be made to match any wedding and add an extra pop of color to your place settings.

MACARON TOWER


Our macaron towers can be used as unique “wedding cakes,” center pieces, or as a special treat for guests on a dessert table or buffet. These macaron masterpieces are completely customizable. You choose the flavors, colors, and number of macarons to suit your wedding party or event. Holds from 37 macarons (four tiers) to 200 macarons (10 tiers).


CUSTOM MACARONS


Want to put your stamp on our macarons? Our personalized service allows you to add an image, logo, design, or text to the tops of our handmade macarons. Your design is printed with edible ink directly onto the tops of our handmade macarons in your chosen flavors and colors. Custom printing is just an additional 0.50¢ per macaron or $26/doz. This price includes a test print so you will see what the final print will look like.
